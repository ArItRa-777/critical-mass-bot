## 🧠 Bot Strategy — Chain Reaction AI

This bot is designed to play **Chain Reaction** using a fast, aggressive, and adaptive decision-making strategy while respecting the competition time constraint of under **1 second per move**.

---

### Core Algorithm

The bot uses the **Minimax algorithm** with **Alpha-Beta pruning** to simulate future game states and select the optimal move.

* **Minimax** allows the bot to evaluate possible future moves by assuming both players play optimally.
* **Alpha-Beta pruning** improves efficiency by eliminating branches that cannot influence the final decision.
* **Iterative Deepening** ensures the bot always returns the best possible move within the time limit.

This combination allows deeper strategic thinking while maintaining fast response time.

---

### Aggressive Playstyle

The bot prioritizes moves that:

* Trigger explosions
* Capture opponent cells
* Create chain reactions
* Expand territory quickly
* Apply constant pressure on the opponent

This aggressive behavior increases the chance of gaining board control and winning through cascading reactions.

---

### Explosion Handling

The bot uses a **queue-based chain reaction system** to resolve explosions efficiently.

Key optimizations:

* Prevents duplicate processing of the same cell
* Ensures stable and fast chain resolution
* Avoids infinite loops using safety limits

This makes the simulation reliable and computationally efficient.

---

### Evaluation Strategy

The bot evaluates board positions using:

* **Orb advantage** — more orbs increases score
* **Cell stability** — cells close to exploding are valuable
* **Board control** — more owned cells improves position
* **Opponent pressure** — attacking unstable enemy cells is prioritized

The goal is to maximize control while minimizing opponent strength.

---

### Performance Optimizations

To meet strict competition timing requirements, the bot includes:

* 1D board representation (faster processing)
* Move ordering (better pruning efficiency)
* Alpha-Beta pruning
* Iterative deepening search
* Optimized explosion resolution
* Time-safe decision making (< 1 second)

These optimizations allow the bot to search deeper game states quickly and reliably.

---

This bot is:

* Strategic

* Competition-ready

It balances speed and intelligence to perform reliably under real-time constraints.
